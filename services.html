Terms of Delivery

We support all standard Incoterms to match your specific trade requirements and risk preferences.

EXW

Ex Works

Buyer assumes all costs and risks from seller's premises.

FOB

Free On Board

Seller delivers goods on board the vessel at the port of shipment.

CIF

Cost, Insurance & Freight

Seller covers cost, insurance, and freight to destination port.

DDP

Delivered Duty Paid

Seller bears all costs and risks until goods reach destination.
