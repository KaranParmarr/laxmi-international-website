# Laxmi International - Activated Carbon

## Overview
This website represents **Laxmi International**, a leading provider of high-quality activated carbon products. It showcases the company's products, services, and applications while providing essential information for potential clients and partners.

## Features
- **Home Page**: Highlights the company's expertise in activated carbon manufacturing and its commitment to quality.
- **About Us**: Details the company's history, mission, and values.
- **Products**: Displays a range of activated carbon products, including coal-based and coconut-based carbon.
- **Services**: Describes the services offered, such as technical support and consultation.
- **Applications**: Explains the various industrial uses of activated carbon, including water treatment, air purification, and pharmaceutical applications.
- **Gallery**: Provides a visual showcase of products and facilities.
- **Contact**: Offers contact details, including phone numbers, email, and location, along with links to social media platforms.

## Purpose
The website aims to:
- Promote **Laxmi International** as a trusted manufacturer and exporter of activated carbon.
- Provide detailed information about the company's products and services.
- Facilitate easy communication with potential clients through contact options and WhatsApp integration.
- Highlight the company's commitment to sustainability and eco-friendly practices.

## Technologies Used
- **HTML5** and **CSS3** for structure and styling.
- **Font Awesome** for icons.
- **Responsive Design** to ensure compatibility across devices.
- **JavaScript** for interactive elements.
